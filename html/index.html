A Look at The Various Ecosystems Found In Nature

Nathan Warner

DGTL 100

This website provides an overview of the diverse ecosystems found around the world, including forests, grasslands, deserts, marine and freshwater environments, tundras, savannas, and mountain ecosystems. We'll explore the unique characteristics of each ecosystem, including animals that call it home, and the benefits that they provide.
