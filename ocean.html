The Ocean Ecosystem

  • Oceans cover about 71% of the Earth's surface.
  • There are five main oceans: Atlantic, Pacific, Indian, Southern, and Arctic.
  • Oceans are home to a wide variety of marine animals, such as fish, whales, dolphins, sharks, and turtles.
  • Oceans play a crucial role in regulating the Earth's climate, absorbing carbon dioxide, and releasing oxygen through phytoplankton.
